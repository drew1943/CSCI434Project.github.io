Helpful Links

Beginner and Intermediate Tutorials

SolveTheCube.com

A clear beginner guide with step-by-step instructions for solving a three by three cube.

J Perm

A well-known tutorial library for everything from beginner lessons to advanced methods including CFOP and blindfolded solving.

CubeSkills

Professional tutorials from top speedcubers with video lessons and written guides.

Ryan's Tutorials

A clear step-by-step walkthrough for solving the cube with a guided approach.

Tools, Timers, Simulators, and Practice Helpers

csTimer

A popular online timer used worldwide for tracking solve times, generating scrambles, and analyzing progress.

Rubikscu.be Simulator

A virtual cube with a solver, scramble generator, interactive tutorial, and built-in timer.

SpeedCubing.com

A collection of algorithms, software tools, and helpful resources for casual and advanced solvers.

SpeedCubeDB

A large database of algorithms covering many puzzles from two by two to six by six and beyond.

Purchasing, Equipment, and Community

GANcube

A well-known manufacturer of smooth, fast-turning speedcubes used by many competitive solvers.

SpeedSolving.com

A global community forum filled with discussions, tips, competitions, and cube related information.
